    Final results — all 3 strategies

    The real tradeoff: FedAvg achieved the highest composite score (0.7304) but also the highest CO₂ (325.7g). The "all" (random selection) strategy had the lowest CO₂ (267.3g) and lowest energy, trading off ~1 point of composite score. FLoRA balances between the two. This is a more honest finding than declaring one strategy strictly best.
